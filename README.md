<h1 align="center">💼 Pool Consultant Management System</h1>

<p align="center">
  🚀 A web-based consultant management platform designed to streamline the lifecycle of bench consultants through intelligent resume management, attendance tracking, skill mapping, training monitoring, and opportunity management.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>📖 Overview</h2>

<p>
The Pool Consultant Management System is a centralized platform that helps organizations efficiently manage consultants who are currently on the bench.
</p>

<p>
The system provides visibility into consultant profiles, technical skills, attendance records, training progress, and project opportunities, enabling better workforce utilization and resource planning.
</p>
